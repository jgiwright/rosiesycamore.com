MY VIOLENT MUSE is a psychological thriller about two female vigilantes whose secret pact to kill prominent male art world figures catches up to them when they start receiving anonymous threats, forcing them to question who they can trust, including each other.
Killing Eve meets Eliza Clark's BOY PARTS

September 2026

Grand Central Publishing/Hachette US

Rosie Sycamore is an Australian author. When not writing or reading, you'll find her in the sun trying to befriend every animal in sight, or re-watching her favourite films and TV shows. MY VIOLENT MUSE is her debut novel.
